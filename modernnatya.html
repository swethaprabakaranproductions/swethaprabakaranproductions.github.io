The Modern Natya Company is hiring additional artists for our home season in May/June 2025!

 

To apply, please submit this Google Form

We will be reviewing applications and reaching out to artists on a rolling basis.

Casting Form

Available Roles:

Dancers  - We're looking for up to 7 dancers with strong foundations in Bharatanatyam technique (or other South Asian classical dance forms). Post-modern and contemporary dance experience is a plus.

Musicians - We’re looking for musicians with strong improvisational and compositional skills to perform live accompaniment.

  • 1 Carnatic vocalist
  • 1 Opera vocalist
  • 1 percussionist - Mridangam, Tabla, Kanjira preferred
  • 1 violinist and/or guitarist – Carnatic experience heavily preferred

**There may be additional opportunities for artists to present some of their own work at the same event. Time slots may range from 5 to 30 minutes. If you would be interested in being considered for this, please use the casting form's "Additional Information" field to write 1 paragraph about what you would be interested in presenting and include a link to 1 additional work sample video (2-5 minutes).**
