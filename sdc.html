Casting Form

Available Roles:

Dancers  - We're looking for 2 dancers with strong foundations in Bharatanatyam technique (or other South Asian classical dance forms). Post-modern and contemporary dance experience is a plus.

Musicians - We’re looking for musicians with strong improvisational and compositional skills to perform live accompaniment.

  • 1 Carnatic vocalist
  • 1 Opera vocalist
  • 1 percussionist - Mridangam, Tabla, Kanjira preferred
  • 1 violinist and/or guitarist – Carnatic experience heavily preferred

Musicians - Rehearsals and Prep Time Commitment:

  • We rehearse on zoom and in-person in SF. All of our in-person rehearsals continue to take diligent COVID-precautions to protect immunocompromised teammates.
  • In-person rehearsals will be held at PUSH dance company in downtown San Francisco (447 Minna St; near 5th & Mission)
  • March:
  • while we’re in the devising process, we would meet max 1-2 times a week. Times/days can potentially be flexible based on what works best for the core team.
  • Hoping to start ASAP, even if it’s quick 30 minute zooms wherever we can all find time
  • April and 1st half of May:
  • lower musician time commitment once show is roughly devised and scored
  • Expect 1 practice/meeting once every 2 weeks or maybe 3 weeks. Would be mostly used for check-ins and “cleanup”
  • We’re also hoping to schedule a studio session to record a high-fidelity version of the music before show day
  • 2nd half of May and June:
  • 1 rehearsal per week minimum with dancers & musicians; used for full runthroughs leading to show day. Max 2 rehearsals/week, if necessary
  • Currently planned for 1 weeknight and 1 weekend session each week, 2-3 hours each time. We’ll determine once we get closer which practices need to be dancers-only vs full company.
