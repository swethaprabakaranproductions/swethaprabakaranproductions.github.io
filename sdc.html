Casting Information:

Dancers  - We're looking for 2 dancers with strong foundations in Bharatanatyam technique. Post-modern and contemporary dance experience is a plus.

Musicians - We’re looking for 1 musician with strong improvisational and compositional skills. Carnatic music or opera experience is a plus.

  • Piano is our preferred instrument, but we're also open to strings players (violin, veena, etc.) and woodwinds (flute, etc).

 

All artists will receive a nominal transportation stipend for this project.


Rehearsals are on Tuesday/Thursday evenings and weekends. We rehearse on zoom and in-person in SF. All of our in-person rehearsals have strict COVID-precautions to protect immunocompromised teammates.
